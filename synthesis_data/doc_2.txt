Title: Can I Delete My Login.gov Or ID.me Account? | Veterans Affairs
Content:
Search resources and support Resources and Support All VA.gov Enter a keyword, phrase, or question Can I delete my Login.gov or ID.me account? Yes. You can delete your Login.gov or ID.me account on the account provider’s website. Deleting your sign-in account won’t delete any information in your VA records. You just won’t be able to use that account to sign in to VA.gov or any other websites.
